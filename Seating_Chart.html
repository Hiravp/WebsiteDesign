Arena Use Rules and Guidelines

General Conduct

Respect Others: Treat all participants, staff, and spectators with courtesy. Disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

No Smoking or Vaping: Smoking and vaping are prohibited in and around the arena premises.

Alcohol Policy: Consumption of alcohol is not allowed unless specifically permitted for certain events.

Facility Use: Cleanliness: Users must keep the area clean and dispose of trash properly.

Equipment Usage: Only authorized personnel may use sound, lighting, and other specialized equipment.

Seating: Sit only in designated seating areas and follow any seating arrangements specified by event organizers.

Emergency Exits: Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and follow evacuation procedures in case of an emergency.

Event-Specific Guidelines:
Tickets: All attendees must have a valid ticket for entry to events. No unauthorized entry will be permitted.

Access to Restricted Areas: Access to backstage, storage, or other restricted areas is prohibited unless authorized.

Personal Items: Attendees are responsible for their personal belongings. The arena is not liable for lost or stolen items.

Safety and Security:
Emergency Protocols: Follow all emergency protocols and instructions from staff during events.

Health and Safety: Adhere to any health and safety guidelines in place, including those related to COVID-19 or other public health concerns.

Rental Guidelines (for those renting the arena):
Booking Procedures: All rental requests must be submitted through the arena planner page and confirmed with a signed contract.

Deposit and Payment: A deposit may be required to secure the booking, with final payment due prior to the event.

Insurance: Renters may be required to provide proof of liability insurance.

Event Setup and Breakdown: Renters are responsible for setting up and breaking down their events within the allotted rental time.

These rules aim to ensure the safety, comfort, and enjoyment of everyone using the arena, whether for events or general use.
