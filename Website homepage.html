About South Forsyth High School


Founded in 1989, South Forsyth High School is located in Cumming, Georgia, a vibrant suburb northeast of Atlanta. Our school is part of the Forsyth County School District and is known for its academic excellence, modern facilities, and rich history. We are committed to providing a top-notch education, offering a wide range of advanced placement courses, fine arts programs, and athletics. With a student body of over 3,000, our diverse community fosters learning, leadership, and personal growth.

  • Established: 1989
  • Student Population: 3,000+
  • Facilities: 4 gyms, state-of-the-art sports fields, science labs, performing arts center
  • Recognition: Ranked among the top public schools in Georgia
  • Programs: AP courses, Career & Technical Education, award-winning clubs, athletics

We pride ourselves on fostering a strong sense of community and providing our students with opportunities to excel in academics, arts, and athletics.

What We Provide: Event Hosting

South Forsyth High School is proud to offer a variety of venues for hosting large events in our state-of-the-art gymnasium and outdoor facilities. Our spaces are perfect for:

  • Graduation Ceremonies
  • Sports Tournaments & Games
  • Birthday Parties & Private Events
  • Live Concerts & Performances
  • Trade Shows & Expos
  • Corporate Events & Conferences
  • Festivals & Cultural Events
  • Fundraisers & Community Gatherings

Our versatile spaces, including gyms, sports fields, and auditoriums, are fully equipped with modern amenities to cater to any event. With ample parking and professional event support, South Forsyth is the ideal venue for your next big event.
